About us:
Founded in 2008 when fintech development was in its infancy, today Tranglo is licensed by the United Kingdom, Singapore, Malaysia and Indonesia as a cross-border payment hub to tackle the inefficiencies of money transfers. Tranglo connects the payments of our business partners and their customers using three main solutions: Tranglo Connect (for money services business licensees), Tranglo Business (for corporates and merchants without money services business licence) and lastly, Tranglo Recharge (an international airtime/data transfer solution that offers cross-border airtime facilities).

Tranglo has an established global network of over 100 countries, 2,500 mobile operators, 1,300 banks/wallets and 130,000 cash pickup points. Many of our partners are fintech giants and unicorns, all of which ride on a unified cross-border payment platform pioneered by Tranglo that makes transactions faster, cheaper and more secure.

Despite the global economic slowdown amid the COVID-19 pandemic, Tranglo has sustained growth through optimisation and digital transformation, proving to have already processed US$1.4 billion in transactions within a span of 6 months compared with previous years.
